Research on C4 Rice using digital image analysis to be presented in National Rice Conference

The research paper entitled Using Digital Image Analysis for Vein Density Measurement in High-throughput Screening for C4 Rice authored by Maria Art Antonette D. Clariño and Vladimir Y. Mariano has been accepted for oral presentation to the 23rd National Rice R&D Conference to be held on 3-5 March 2010 at the International Rice Research Institute in Los Baños, Laguna. Likewise, the paper A Molecular Dynamics Metaphor for Optimizing the Cultivar Coefficients in CERES-Rice by Jaderick P. Pabico and Jose Rene L. Micor has been accepted for poster presentation in the same conference.
